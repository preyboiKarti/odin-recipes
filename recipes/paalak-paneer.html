Paalak Paneer

Description:

Palak Paneer is one of the most popular Indian dish made with succulent paneer cubes (Indian cottage cheese) in a smooth spinach sauce. There are two ways to make this healthy dish â€“ a homestyle version and a restaurant style version with a charcoal smoking method. Both the vibrant green palak paneer recipes are made with fresh spinach leaves (palak in Hindi), aromatics, herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chili
  • Black Salt
  • Garam Masala
  • Kasoori Methi
  • Heavy Cream
  • Paneer
  • Cashews

Steps:

  1. Make onion puree and keep it aside.
  2. Blanch spinach and make puree.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot add tomatoes.
  4. Mash the tomatoes and cook until they're soft.
  5. Add prepared onion puree.
  6. Mix and simmer for 2-3 min.
  7. Add remaining salt, black salt, garam masala and crushed kasoori methi.
  8. Mix well.
  9. Add prepared spinach puree and water and mix well.
  10. Add paneer and heavy cream.
  11. Mix well and let the gravy come to a simmer and turn off the stove right away. Palak paneer is ready to serve.
